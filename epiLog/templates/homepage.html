epiLog

epiLog is a digital humanities project that allows abstraction, annotation and comparison of user-defined "objects". These "objects", be they books, films or performance art pieces are broken down into manageable units and annotated by users. epiLog then allows cross-object comparisons.

Learn more »

Free, Open Source

epiLog is free, Open Source software, released under the GPL v3. You are free to download, use and change it so long as you adhere to these terms.

Share and Browse

Your annotations can be private or public, but the biggest value to the community occurs when your objects are made public so that others can cross-compare.

Get started today

If you just want to get annotating, begin a class project or your own research then it's easy to get started.
